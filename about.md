# Prompt for CMS Setup (Short)

Build a CMS for the Toe Tripper website with these pages and content types:

## Home
- Hero: title, subtitle, CTA label, CTA link, background image.
- Services preview: 3-6 cards (title, short text, icon).
- Metrics: 3-5 items (label, value).
- Blog preview: 3 posts (title, summary, image, link).
- Newsletter CTA: title, short text, email placeholder.
- Footer: address, phone, email, social links.

## About
- Story: title, body, featured image.
- Team: people (name, role, photo, short bio).
- Values or metrics: 3-6 items (label, value).

## Packages
- Hero: title, subtitle, CTA label, CTA link.
- Package list: items (name, price, short description, features list).
- Info blocks: title, short text.

## Services
- Hero: title, subtitle, CTA label, CTA link.
- Services list: items (title, short description, icon).
- Features: 3-6 items (title, short text).
